Students take to technology quite naturally, but what they need is disciplined guidance to turn their aptitude into a skill or qualification. Our Computer Lab does more than just introduce students to the fundamentals of computer science. Through a hands-on approach, we train students in the concepts of coding, web browsing, and designing.
